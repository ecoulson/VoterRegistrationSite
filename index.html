Why Should I Register To Vote?

U.S. high school students are getting organized and politically active. It's not about what party you support or where you land on an issue.​ It’s that you must vote to be heard.
Organizing a school voter registration drive at your school is a great opportunity to register newly eligible voters and engage your classmates in the voting process.
For the past three decades voters have been disproportionately of higher income, older, or more partisan in their interests, yet young voters account for nearly HALF OF THE VOTING POPULATION, making YOU a powerful political force!

# Pre Registering

One of they key components of being able to vote is making sure that you are of age! If you are not of the legal voting age you can still register to vote in some states. This all depends on the legislation and programs in place within the state you currently reside in. To find out if you can register to vote even if you are not of voting age check out the link below!
